About R.E.C Pratapgarh

Established in 2023, the Rajkiya engineering College Pratapgarh (R.E.C PRATAPGARH) is one of India's premier institutions for higher education, engineering, science, and technology. Located in Pratapgarh, Uttar Pradesh, the campus spans over 25 acres, offering a vibrant and intellectually stimulating environment for students, researchers, and faculty.

Key Highlights

  • World-Class Academics: Offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across diverse disciplines.
  • Research Excellence: Cutting-edge labs and partnerships drive innovation in AI, renewable energy, and more.
  • Innovation and Entrepreneurship: Home to the Startup Incubation and Innovation Centre (SIIC).
  • Campus Life: A vibrant community with world-class facilities for holistic development.
