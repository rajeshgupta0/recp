Welcome

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Rajkiya Engineering Pratapgarh (REC Pratapgarh) was the first department in India to start Computer Science education. It started in August 2023 with an IBM 1620 system - a novelty then, even in many North American and European universities. The department runs BTech, MTech, MS and PhD programs and is engaged in cutting edge research with sponsored projects and consultancies. Many of the nation's leading experts, educationists and consultants in computer science today are the alumni of this department.
